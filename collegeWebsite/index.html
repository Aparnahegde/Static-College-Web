Welcome

MOTENSAR MEMORIAL ARTS AND SCIENCE COLLEGE SIRSI, was formerly known as M M ARTS AND SCIENCE COLLEGE SIRSI is the mother of all constituent schools and colleges of Bapuji Educational Association. Previously it was a Government intermediate college started in 1947 and was run by the Government of Mysore. The Bapuji Educational Association took over this college in 1958. (Perhaps first of its kind) and converted in to A First Grade Arts, Commerce and Science college. Being the only college in and around SIRSI. It catered to the educational needs of people both rural and urban. It is named after the donor Sri Dharma Rathnakara Rajanahalli Maddurayappa, (DRM) SIRSI

The Government of Karnataka took a decision to separate P.U. classes from Composite Degree College. Accordingly the college was further bifurcated into DRM PU Science College and DRM Science College in 2001.

The other details regarding admissions, programmes offered and fee structure etc., are given in the College Prospectus.
